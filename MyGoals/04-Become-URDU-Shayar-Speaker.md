## üéì Shayari Selection

### Top Shayars to Study
1. **Mirza Ghalib (1797-1869)**
   - Modern Urdu poetry
   - Philosophical and romantic themes
   - Innovative use of language

2. **Allama Iqbal (1877-1938)**
   - Revolutionary and philosophical poetry
   - National poet of Pakistan
   - Khudi-e-Urdu (The Anthem of the East)

3. **Faiz Ahmed Faiz (1911-1984)**
   - Progressive and revolutionary
   - Social and political themes
   - Popular with younger generation

4. **Ahmad Faraz (1931-1934)**
   - Romantic and devotional poetry
   - Classical traditions
   - Widely appreciated

### Types of Shayari to Master

**1. Ghazal**
   - 5-15 lines, matla form
   - Rhyming word in final line
   - Focus on beloved (praise of Prophet Muhammad PBUH)

**2. Nazm**
   - Short verse, narrative style
   - Longer than ghazal but free form
   - Can be satirical or serious

**3. Rubai**
   - Four-line quatrain, AABB rhyme
   - Philosophical or romantic themes
   - Popular form for expression

**4. Marsiya**
   - Elegiac poem, mourning form
   - Themes of loss and grief
   - Serious and emotional

**5. Doha**
   - Two-line verse, satirical or humorous
   - Wisdom or advice
   - Social commentary
